How it works

1
We add one script tag to your website. Works on WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, or any custom site. No software to install, no training required.
2
A customer starts an enquiry on your site. Instead of a contact form, they have a conversation with the AI. It asks the exact questions your engineers would ask — adapted for your industry.
3
Your sales team receives a structured RFQ. Specs, contact details, drawings, timeline, compliance requirements — all captured, scored, and emailed to your inbox. Ready to quote.
