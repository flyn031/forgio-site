Questions

Things people ask.

How quickly can we go live?
Most customers are live within 48 hours. You add one line of code to your website, we configure the AI for your industry, and you're taking enquiries.
Does it work on Wix / WordPress / Squarespace?
Yes. The widget is a single JavaScript tag. It works on any website platform that lets you add custom code to the header — which is all of them.
What happens to the data?
Enquiry data is yours. RFQs are emailed directly to your team. We don't sell data or share it with anyone. Hosted on UK/EU infrastructure.
Can I try it before committing?
Yes — visit our demos page and have a real conversation with Forgio configured for your industry. No signup needed. Or book a 20-minute call and we'll walk you through it.
Is there a contract?
No. All plans are monthly with no minimum term. Cancel any time — though once you see the structured RFQs arriving, you won't want to.
What industries do you support?
We specialise in industrial and engineering businesses — conveyor manufacturers, refrigeration contractors, fabricators, process equipment makers. If you quote bespoke projects, we're built for you.
