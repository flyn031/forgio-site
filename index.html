How it works

An always-on technical sales coordinator for every enquiry.

Forgio drops into your website in minutes. From there, it does what your best sales engineer would do — gathers specs, asks the right follow-ups, and delivers a structured brief your estimators can price immediately.

01 / Intake

Customer starts an enquiry

No more empty textbox. Forgio greets them, asks what they need, and immediately begins routing into the right technical question flow.

02 / Qualify

AI asks the right questions

Dimensions, materials, environment, timeline, decision-maker. It adapts to the answers, requests drawings where needed, and never misses a critical spec.

03 / Deliver

Estimators receive a scoped brief

A structured RFQ lands in your inbox — requirements, materials, compliance notes, timelines, site constraints, lead score, and a list of anything still missing. Your engineers can start pricing immediately.
