How it works

A junior pre-sales engineer
on every enquiry.

Forgio embeds on your site as a single line of code. From there, it does what your best sales engineer would do — only it never sleeps and never forgets to ask.

01 / Intake

Customer starts an enquiry

No more empty textbox. Forgio greets them, asks what they need, and immediately begins routing into the right technical question flow.

02 / Qualify

AI asks the right questions

Dimensions, materials, environment, timeline, decision-maker. It adapts to the answers, requests drawings where needed, and never misses a critical spec.

03 / Deliver

Sales receives a structured RFQ

A clean brief lands in your inbox or CRM — specs, attachments, missing fields flagged, lead score attached. Ready for your engineers to quote.
