— Capabilities

From steel frames to precision assemblies.

We work across structural, architectural, and bespoke fabrication — mild steel, stainless, and aluminium. CE/UKCA marked to BS EN 1090, with coded welders and full NDT capability.

Structural steelwork

Portal frames, mezzanines, trusses, columns and beams. BS EN 1090 EXC2/3/4.

Architectural metalwork

Balustrades, staircases, canopies, feature steelwork. Polished, brushed or powder-coated finishes.

Bespoke fabrication

Tanks, hoppers, chutes, guards, enclosures, brackets. One-offs and small batches.

Sheet metal

Laser cutting, folding, punching and welded assemblies. Mild steel, stainless and aluminium.

Coded & pressure work

Pressure vessels, pipework, coded welding to PD 5500 and ASME VIII.

Site installation

Erection, modification, and repair. CSCS-carded teams, RAMS and lift plans provided.
