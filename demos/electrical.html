— What we do

Industrial electrical from design to commissioning.

From MCC panel builds to full ATEX hazardous area installations, we handle every aspect of industrial electrical work — designed, installed, and certified to BS 7671:2018.

⚙️

Control panel builds

MCC, PLC, VSD, distribution boards and bespoke enclosures. Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Schneider, Mitsubishi. CE marked to BS EN 61439.

⚡

Site installation

Cable installation, SWA, LSOH, FP200, MICC. Cable tray, ladder, trunking, conduit. Complete terminations and labelling to client schedules.

🔥

ATEX hazardous areas

Zone 1 and Zone 2 gas installations. Zone 21 and 22 dust environments. Ex-rated equipment selection, installation and certification to IECEx/ATEX.

🖥️

PLC & control systems

PLC programming, HMI design, SCADA integration, instrumentation, fieldbus — Profibus, Profinet, DeviceNet, EtherNet/IP.

🔍

Testing & inspection

18th edition periodic inspection (EICR), initial verification, thermographic surveys, PAT testing, insurance inspections.

📋

Planned maintenance

PPM contracts for industrial sites. Electrical switchgear servicing, thermographic inspection, infrared surveys, annual test and inspection.
